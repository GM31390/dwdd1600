Our story

I was born at a very young age, this affected me so greatly I didn't speak to my parents for 2 years

eventually when I got older I got into baking through watching how 2 basic videos. that was the birthplace of our coveted blue strawberries

after an incedint involving mustard gas and a toilet, my mother took me to train under gordan ramsey.

now with the help of my leagally questionable father, i started this bakery to share my skills with the world.
